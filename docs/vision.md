# CareerOS

## Mission

Build an AI-powered Career Operating System that helps students and freshers discover opportunities, prepare for interviews, track progress, and make informed career decisions.

## Target Users

- Students
- Freshers
- Internship seekers
- Job seekers

## Core Pillars

1. Dashboard
2. Opportunity Explorer
3. Community Intelligence
4. Career Copilot

## Goals

- Reduce career confusion
- Centralize career information
- Provide salary transparency
- Improve interview preparation
- Offer AI-driven recommendations
